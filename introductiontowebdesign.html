Introduction to Web Design

What is Webdesign ?

Webdesign is a process of publishing your content on Internet by making Website. In other word, A web designer creates the layout and design of website to make the website best in look. It include creating, uploading, updating, and posting the content of web pages such as text, images, graphs, videos etc.
